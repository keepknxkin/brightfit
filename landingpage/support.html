Before You Email

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

  • A brief description of the problem

  • Screenshots, if applicable

Subscriptions & Billing

BrightFit Plus subscriptions are managed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. To cancel, change, or request a refund, you may need to go through your device's subscription settings or the store where you purchased.

If you're having trouble with a subscription, email us at nathan.brightfit1@gmail.com and include the email associated with your store account.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data BrightFit collects and how we use it.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Introduction

BrightFit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the BrightFit mobile application and related services.

By using BrightFit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, or other details you provide when creating an account or contacting support.
  • Fitness and workout data — including workout history, exercise logs, routines, progress metrics, achievements, and goals you set within the app.
  • Device information — such as device type, operating system version, app version, and general technical identifiers needed to run and improve the app.
  • Usage data — including how you interact with features, screens viewed, and general app performance data.
  • Subscription and purchase data — if you subscribe to BrightFit Plus, payment and subscription status are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your full payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve your workout experience
  • Track your fitness progress and display achievements
  • Process subscriptions and manage BrightFit Plus features
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you
  • Fix bugs, improve performance, and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on your device and, where applicable, on secure servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

BrightFit may use third-party services to help operate the app, including payment processors (Apple and Google), analytics tools, and subscription management providers. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app, under confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of BrightFit, our users, or others
  • With your consent

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at nathan.brightfit1@gmail.com. You may also delete the app from your device at any time.

Children's Privacy

BrightFit is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of BrightFit after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at nathan.brightfit1@gmail.com.
